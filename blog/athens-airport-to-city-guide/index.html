✈️ Complete Guide: Athens Airport to City Center Transportation

October 20, 2025

Your complete guide to getting from Eleftherios Venizelos Airport to Athens city center. All transport options, costs, and booking links for a hassle-free journey.

Getting from Eleftherios Venizelos Airport to the vibrant heart of Athens is easier than you think. Here's a simple guide with all the transport options and up-to-date costs, plus direct links so you can book or grab tickets hassle-free.

🚕 Take a Taxi for a Smooth Ride

If convenience is your priority, official taxis are available 24/7 right outside the arrivals area (Exit 3). The journey takes about 35 to 40 minutes depending on traffic.

Cost: €40 during the day (5:00 am to midnight), €60 at night (midnight to 5:00 am), flat rate including luggage and tolls.

Good to know: Easy door-to-door service, especially handy if you have luggage or arrive late.

Book or learn more: Athens Airport Taxi Official

🚇 Speedy Metro (Line 3 - Blue Line)

For a quick and budget-friendly option, jump on the Metro that runs straight from the airport to Syntagma Square, just a short walk from Plaka.

Cost: €9 per person one-way.

Duration: About 40 minutes.

Hours: Approximately 6:10 am to 11:34 pm.

Where to buy tickets: Ticket machines at the station or online info at Athens Metro Tickets.

🚌 Budget Express Bus (X95 Line)

The X95 bus is the cheapest way into the city and runs 24/7, great if you arrive very early or late.

Cost: €5.50 per ticket.

Duration: Around 60 minutes, depending on traffic.

Boarding: The bus stop is just outside the arrivals hall.

Tickets & info: Athens Airport Bus Tickets

🚗 Private Transfers: Comfort & Peace of Mind

If you want everything arranged before you land, private transfers offer personalized, hassle-free service with a driver waiting for you.

Cost: Starting from about €43, varies by vehicle type and provider.

Duration: Around 25 to 40 minutes.

Book now: Options at Travel Transfer or Welcome Pickups.

🚙 Renting a Car

For maximum flexibility, consider renting a car right at the airport. This is perfect if you're planning to explore beyond Athens or arriving with family.

💡 Handy Tips

  • Heading out late or traveling with lots of bags? Take a taxi or private transfer for ease.

  • If you want a quick ride without breaking the bank, the metro is your best bet.

  • Bus X95 is perfect for budget travelers arriving or departing at odd hours.

  • For extra peace of mind, pre-book private transfers, especially for groups or families.
